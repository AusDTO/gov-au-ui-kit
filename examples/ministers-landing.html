About the minister

Senator the Hon Mitch Fifield

Liberal Party of Australia

Senator Fifield was appointed Minister for Communications and Minister for the Arts on 21 September 2015.

More about the minister

Responsibilities

The minister leads the Department of Communications and the Arts and other agencies within the government's Communications portfolio.

The minister works with the community, government organisations and professional associations to create and update communication laws.
